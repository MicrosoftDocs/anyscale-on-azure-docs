# Support model

Anyscale on Azure support is handled through Azure Support. Whether your issue is related to the Azure infrastructure, AKS configuration, or the Anyscale platform itself, open a support request through the Azure portal and the support team will route your case appropriately.

> [!NOTE]
> Customers who have direct support channels set up with Anyscale Support may choose to contact Anyscale Support directly for Anyscale platform issues. Anyscale on Azure support SLAs are at the [Enterprise tier](https://www.anyscale.com/support) at launch.

## How to get support

1. Sign in to the [Azure portal](https://portal.azure.com).
2. Select **Portal menu** in the upper-left corner, then select **Help + support** at the bottom of the navigation panel.
3. Select **Create a support request** and follow the prompts.

For guidance on opening Azure support requests, see [Create an Azure support request](/azure/azure-portal/supportability/how-to-create-azure-support-request).

## Support scope during Public Preview

Anyscale on Azure is currently in Public Preview. Support during Public Preview is provided on a best-effort basis. For information on Azure support plans and their coverage, see [Azure Support Plans](https://azure.microsoft.com/support/plans/).
